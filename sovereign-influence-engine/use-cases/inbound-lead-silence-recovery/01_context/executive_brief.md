# Executive Brief

## What is being deployed
A minimum viable influence engine for CX/sales that automatically recovers silent leads after outreach, demo, or pricing events.

## Why this is the correct first production use case
- high frequency
- clear measurable ROI
- low integration complexity
- narrow enough for fast deployment
- aligned with the existing SIE doctrine that emphasizes governed action and outcome calibration

## Business outcome
Convert silence into re-engagement:
- reply
- meeting booked
- revived opportunity
- closed revenue

## Strategic significance
This deployment validates the full causal chain:
signal -> decision -> execution -> outcome -> calibration

That is the smallest live proof that the SIE is real.
